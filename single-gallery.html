Everything About the OnePlus 9 Series.

  • September 06, 2020
  • Design

The fans of Oneplus are having a whale of a time as the most expected official launch of the OnePlus 9 series has finally taken place. Even though the anticipation was agonizing, it was worth the wait after we got to watch the launch event. Each and everything we witnessed in the promotional event was mind-blowing. The event explained all the features and upgrades with close attention to details.


Here are all the OnePlus 9 series features and specifications in detail for those who were waiting for them.
The launch event introduced three new stunning smartphones: The OnePlus 9 Pro, the OnePlus 9, and the OnePlus 9R.
Colour
The OnePlus 9 Pro is available in three unique colours: Morning Mist, Pine Green, and Stellar Black. These are gorgeous colours designed with inspiration from nature. The Morning Mist is a combination of silver and black. The central idea behind the colour formation is the fading of fog on a glossy mirror. The Pine Green colour has a matte finish making the glass so soft. The Stellar Black colour comes with special matte frosted glass that traces no fingerprint.

The OnePlus 9 is available in Winter Mist, Astral Black, Arctic Sky. The Winter Mist is on the idea of the early winter morning, Astral Black on the bluish darkish sky, and the Arctic Sky has a bluish-grey colour similar to sky blue. The OnePlus 9R comes in two different colours: Lake Blue and Carbon Black.
