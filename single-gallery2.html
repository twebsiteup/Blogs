Apple-Airpods-Pro-True-Wireless The Apple AirPods Pro exhilarates high-fidelity audio and creates effortless magic to your ears. You can experience the ultimate personal listening with the AirPods Pro’s Active Noise Cancellation & Transparency modes.
Want to switch Active Noise Cancellation to Transparency mode instantly? Don’t worry! Just press the force sensor to move between Active Noise Cancellation and Transparency Mode. You can also use the button to control music.
The Apple AirPods Pro comes with the Apple H1 headphone chip. A single charge gives you around 4.5 hours of listening time. So, charge your AirPods Pro for just 5 minutes, and it gives you about 1 hour of listening time.
It uses Bluetooth version 5.0 to connect to almost any smartphone or laptop, providing rich audio and heart-thumping bass. The Bluetooth range is about 10m.
This product has unique features like an adaptive Equalizer, a Vent system for pressure equalization, a Custom high-excursion Apple driver, and a Custom high dynamic range amplifier.
